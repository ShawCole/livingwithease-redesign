Recommendations

Proposed Changes

Based on our analysis of the current site, Jayne's positioning, market trends, and best practices for transformational coaching websites.

1

Redesign the Homepage Hero — Lead with the Pain Point

HIGH IMPACT — Conversion

The current hero repeats "Feeling Stuck?" 10 times in the source code (likely a rendering issue) and then jumps into explanation. The new hero should immediately validate the visitor's struggle, present Jayne as the guide, and give one clear CTA.

Current
  • "Feeling Stuck?" repeated many times
  • Long paragraph before any CTA
  • No clear visual hierarchy
  • Generic stock hero image
Proposed
  • Single powerful headline + subhead
  • Professional photo of Jayne
  • "Schedule a Discovery Call" CTA above the fold
  • Social proof snippet (11 testimonials)
2

Consolidate Fonts (12+ down to 2)

HIGH IMPACT — Performance + Visual Consistency

The current site loads Adamina, Poppins, Josefin Slab, Cantarell, Abril Fatface, Playfair Display, Open Sans, Cinzel, Source Sans Pro, Yeseva One, Lato, and Droid Sans. This dramatically slows page load and creates visual chaos. We recommend Playfair Display for headings and Poppins for body text — both already in use, just not consistently.

Current
  • 12+ font families loading
  • Inconsistent type hierarchy
  • Slow page loads
Proposed
  • 2 font families (Playfair + Poppins)
  • Clear heading/body hierarchy
  • Much faster page loads
3

Create a Clear Client Journey / Services Architecture

HIGH IMPACT — Clarity + Conversion

The current site has "Soul Aligned Reset" and "Beyond Manifestation" as separate nav items, but it's not immediately clear how they relate, which is the entry point, or what the investment looks like. We propose a unified Services page that presents the clear journey: Discovery Call → Soul Aligned Reset (4 sessions) → Deeper Work.

Current
  • Services split across multiple pages
  • No pricing transparency
  • Unclear entry point
  • "Beyond Manifestation" page 404s
Proposed
  • Unified "Work With Me" page
  • Visual journey map / timeline
  • Clear pricing or "starting at" ranges
  • Single entry point: Discovery Call
4

Elevate the Testimonials with Visual Design

MEDIUM IMPACT — Trust + Social Proof

Jayne has 11 powerful, detailed testimonials — this is a major asset. Currently they're presented as a simple list. We propose redesigning them as a visual story with client photos (where available), pull quotes, and categorized results (physical health, relationships, career, emotional freedom).

Current
  • Plain text list of testimonials
  • Long and overwhelming
  • No visual hierarchy
Proposed
  • Card-based layout with pull quotes
  • Category filters (health, career, etc.)
  • Featured testimonial carousel on homepage
  • Results metrics highlighted
5

Add a Lead Magnet / Email Capture Funnel

HIGH IMPACT — Lead Generation

The site currently mentions a free e-book "Balance Your Chakras, Balance Your Life!" but there's no prominent opt-in. We propose adding a dedicated lead magnet section with a compelling opt-in above the fold, automated email nurture sequence, and a resource library page to build the email list.

Current
  • Contact form only captures warm leads
  • No nurture sequence
  • Free e-book buried or missing
Proposed
  • Prominent chakra guide opt-in
  • Pop-up or slide-in on exit intent
  • Email nurture sequence (5-7 emails)
  • Free resources page for SEO
6

Migrate Off GoDaddy Website Builder

HIGH IMPACT — Performance + Flexibility

GoDaddy Website Builder is limiting. The heavy JS rendering, poor SEO control, and template constraints prevent the site from performing at its potential. We recommend migrating to a custom-built static site deployed on Netlify — fast, modern, and fully customizable.

Current
  • GoDaddy Website Builder
  • JS-rendered (poor SEO)
  • 12+ font files loading
  • Template constraints
Proposed
  • Custom HTML/CSS on Netlify
  • Lightning-fast static site
  • Perfect SEO control
  • Full design freedom
7

Rewrite the About Page as a Story

MEDIUM IMPACT — Connection + Trust

The current About page is good but reads more like a professional bio. For a transformational coach, the About page should be the most emotionally compelling page on the site — Jayne's personal story of transformation IS the proof that her method works. We propose restructuring it as a narrative arc: struggle → discovery → transformation → mission.

Current
  • Professional but somewhat clinical
  • Methodology-focused
  • Missing personal photos
Proposed
  • Narrative arc: struggle → mission
  • Professional headshot + lifestyle photos
  • Credentials woven into story
  • Ends with CTA to work together
8

Add Calendly Integration + Simplified Contact

MEDIUM IMPACT — Conversion

The current contact page has a basic form and mentions WhatsApp. The Calendly link exists but isn't prominently featured. We propose making the Calendly booking the PRIMARY CTA across the entire site, embedded directly rather than linked externally.

Current
  • Basic contact form
  • Calendly linked but not embedded
  • "We'll get back in 1 business day"
Proposed
  • Embedded Calendly on dedicated page
  • "Schedule a Session" in header nav
  • Floating CTA button on every page
  • WhatsApp kept as secondary option
