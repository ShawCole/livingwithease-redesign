Design Direction

This proposal follows an art-directed, narrative-driven approach — not a template-based redesign. Inspired by the kind of bespoke, emotionally resonant work Jamie Crowley builds for her clients, the new site will feature:

  • Distinctive, intentional typography (not generic Google Fonts defaults)
  • Full-bleed imagery with authentic photography as the emotional core
  • Percentage-based spacing for a proportional, editorial feel at every viewport
  • A restrained color palette (3-4 accents) with warm neutrals
  • Narrative flow — storytelling copy that guides visitors through a journey
  • Subtle, purposeful animation (scroll reveals, hover states) not decorative motion
  • Every page art-directed individually, not stamped from one layout

Recommendations

Proposed Changes

Eight strategic improvements based on our analysis of the current site, Jayne's positioning, market research, and best practices for art-directed wellness coaching websites.

01

Art-Directed Homepage Hero

High Impact — Conversion

The current hero repeats "Feeling Stuck?" multiple times (likely a rendering bug) and uses a generic stock image. The new hero should be a full-bleed, cinematic moment — Jayne's professional photo, a single powerful headline, and one clear CTA. Art-directed with intentional typography hierarchy and atmospheric color treatment, not a template hero section.

Current
  • "Feeling Stuck?" repeated in source
  • Long paragraph before any CTA
  • No visual hierarchy
  • Generic stock hero image
Proposed
  • Full-bleed hero with Jayne's photo
  • Single editorial headline + subhead
  • "Begin Your Recalibration" CTA above fold
  • Social proof snippet from 11 testimonials
02

Distinctive Typography System

High Impact — Brand Identity

A custom or distinctive typeface is the single most impactful art-direction decision — it does more for brand identity than any other design choice. We'll consolidate the current 12+ fonts into a two-family system: a refined serif for headlines (Cormorant Garamond or a custom face) and a clean sans-serif for body (Outfit). This alone will transform how the site feels.

Current
  • 12+ font families loading simultaneously
  • Inconsistent type hierarchy page to page
  • Slow page loads from font bloat
  • No typographic identity
Proposed
  • 2 intentional font families
  • Clear display/body hierarchy
  • Dramatically faster page loads
  • Recognizable brand voice in every headline
03

Unified Client Journey Architecture

High Impact — Clarity

The current site splits services across "Soul Aligned Reset" and "Beyond Manifestation" (which 404s) with no clear entry point or investment range. We propose a single art-directed "Work With Me" page that presents the client journey as a narrative: Discovery Call → Soul Aligned Reset (4 sessions) → Deeper Transformation — with visual timeline and pricing transparency.

Current
  • Services split across multiple pages
  • No pricing transparency
  • Unclear entry point for new clients
  • "Beyond Manifestation" page broken (404)
Proposed
  • Single "Work With Me" narrative page
  • Visual journey map / timeline
  • Clear pricing or "starting at" ranges
  • One entry point: Discovery Call
04

Testimonials as Narrative Stories

Medium Impact — Trust

11 powerful, detailed testimonials are Jayne's strongest asset — but they're buried in a plain text list. We'll redesign them as art-directed story cards with pull quotes, result highlights, and category tags. A featured testimonial carousel on the homepage, plus a dedicated stories page with alternating layouts (like the L.Y.D.I.A. Foundation's portfolio pages) that lets each client's transformation breathe.

Current
  • Plain text wall of testimonials
  • Long and overwhelming to scroll
  • No visual hierarchy or categorization
Proposed
  • Art-directed story cards with pull quotes
  • Category filters (health, career, relationships)
  • Alternating narrative layouts per story
  • Featured carousel on homepage
05

Lead Magnet & Email Nurture Funnel

High Impact — Lead Gen

Jayne has a free e-book "Balance Your Chakras, Balance Your Life!" but there's no prominent opt-in anywhere on the site. We propose an art-directed lead capture section with atmospheric design, a compelling value proposition, and automated email nurture sequence (5-7 emails) that warms prospects toward booking a Discovery Call.

Current
  • Contact form only captures warm leads
  • No nurture sequence
  • Free e-book buried or missing entirely
Proposed
  • Prominent chakra guide opt-in on every page
  • Exit-intent or scroll-triggered capture
  • Automated email nurture to Discovery Call
  • Free resources page for organic SEO
06

Migrate Off GoDaddy to Custom Build

High Impact — Performance

GoDaddy Website Builder is the ceiling on this site's potential. Heavy JS rendering kills SEO, template constraints prevent art direction, and the bloated font loading slows everything down. We'll build a custom static site on Netlify — lightning fast, fully art-directed, perfect SEO control, and zero template constraints.

Current
  • GoDaddy Website Builder (template)
  • JS-rendered pages (poor SEO)
  • 12+ unnecessary font files
  • Template layout constraints
Proposed
  • Custom HTML/CSS on Netlify CDN
  • Sub-second page loads
  • Full SEO control (meta, schema, sitemap)
  • Complete design freedom per page
07

About Page as Origin Story

Medium Impact — Connection

For a transformational coach, the About page IS the proof. Jayne's personal journey — from Wall Street to speech pathology to energy healing — is the most compelling content on the site. We'll restructure it as a full-bleed narrative arc: the struggle, the discovery, the transformation, the mission. Professional and lifestyle photography, not stock imagery. Each section art-directed as its own moment.

Current
  • Professional but clinical tone
  • Methodology-focused, not story-focused
  • No professional photography
Proposed
  • Narrative arc: struggle → discovery → mission
  • Full-bleed section photography
  • Credentials woven naturally into story
  • Ends with CTA to work together
08

Embedded Calendly as Primary CTA

Medium Impact — Conversion

The existing Calendly link works but isn't prominent. We'll make booking the primary action across the entire site: embedded scheduling on a dedicated page, a persistent "Book a Discovery Call" in the header, and contextual CTAs after every section. WhatsApp remains as a secondary warm contact option.

Current
  • Basic contact form as primary
  • Calendly linked but not embedded
  • "We'll get back in 1 business day"
Proposed
  • Embedded Calendly on dedicated page
  • "Book a Call" in global header
  • Contextual CTAs after every section
  • WhatsApp as warm secondary option
